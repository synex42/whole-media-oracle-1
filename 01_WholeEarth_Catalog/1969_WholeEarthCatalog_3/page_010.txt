corner newsstand, or even in your favorite camera store.

black-and-white photography exclusively, and all have a 
fresh, contemporary outlook, there are no technical tips 
or how-to-do-it pieces. But you will find nicely 
reproduced portolios of today’s photographers, 
and sometimes one of yesterday’s photographers.
