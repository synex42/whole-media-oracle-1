The CoEvolution Quarterly is the Whole Earth Catalog’s new format. The CQ carries on 
the Catalog’s research in a somewhat deeper vein, offering more of what CQ and Catalog 
editor Stewart Brand calls “conceptual news.” Access to tools — reviews of books, tools 
and techniques — is still provided in the traditional Catalog categories. 144 full pages (no 
advertising), color cover, illustrations. From the CQ, Box 428, Sausalito, CA 94965.
