T-D loom makes a good beginning weaving project.

sculpturing and in educating kids — you can let them work forever 
without having the.stuff dry out in their hands. The softest grade

together some #1 and #2, in equal parts. Then when a kid puts 
together a masterpiece, you can either let it stand as is or make a 
casting of-it — Plastilina is especially good for rubber moulding. 
It’s also. completely recycleable— as they say, it never dries out, so 
there’s no hassle about storing it airtightly. It does, aie get better
