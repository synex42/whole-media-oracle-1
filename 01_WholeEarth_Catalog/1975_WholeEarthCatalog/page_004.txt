The UPDATED Catalog 
THE LAST WHOLE EARTH CATALOG was put together between 
1968 and 1971. 
(It is called “The Last” because we stopped our en- 
tire business of research in May 1971 — permanently, we thought.) 
We resumed business in September 1973, beginning with an entire 
update of the CATALOG. Pam Cokeley contacted every one of 
the 2,100 listings, finding that over half of them required some 
change in price, address, etc. 
Those 3,000 changes went into the 
13th, 14th, and 15th editions. 
With the 16th edition — done in the Spring of 1975 — a fresh 
entire update was run, along with new covers, with subjects cross- 
referenced to the WHOLE EARTH EPILOG (see below), a com- 
bined index to CATALOG and EPILOG, and some bonus articles 
(p. 131). 
In a few cases items that are now defunct or out of 
print have been replaced by new items. Mostly, though, we let 
the skeletons lie there — for honor, sorrow, or warning. 
(And 
out-of-print books are readily findable in libraries.) 
Preparation of new editions is now done entirely at our office — 
for better thoroughness and consistency. 
Distribution has been 
transferred from Random House to Penguin Books. 
The update 
staff on the 16th edition was J.D. Smith (supervisor); Pam Cokeley, 
Andrea Sharp and Diana Barich (corrections); Jeanne Campbell 
and Diana Fairbanks (paste-up); Andrew Main (graphic coordina- 
tion); Joy Byars (typesetting); and Stewart Brand (me — editor). 
items in the CATALOG are not re-evaluated with each new edi- 
tion — that goes on in the EPILOG and the CO-EVOLUTION 
QUARTERLY 
(see below). But the updating of access (price, 
address, etc.) in the CATALOG goes on — current to.the most 
recent edition (these access changes are also 
noted in the CO-EVOLUTION QUARTERLY).

EPILOG 
The EPILOG is a continuation of THE (UPDATED) LAST WHOLE 
EARTH CATALOG — in effect Volume II. It commences where 
the CATALOG leaves off, with page 449, and comprises all new 
material, cross-referenced and cross-indexed with the CATALOG. 
The EPILOG research, somewhat higher quality than the 
CATALOG’s, was dane in 1973 and 1974. Each successive 
edition is. completely updated. 
The EPIL OG is 320 pages, costs $4 in paperback ($9.25 in hard- 
cover), and is available in bookstores or (postpaid) from Whole 
Earth Truck Store. 
The distributor is Penguin Books, 625 Madison 
Ave., New York, NY 10022.
