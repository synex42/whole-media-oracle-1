Uses Manure Both as Fertilizer and Medicine 
To keep my flower garden looking good, | use another 
natural resource, manure, both as a fertilizer and a medicine 
for every plant in our yard. | prefer horse manure for its 
light texture, and it makes a very nice mulch when dry. If a 
plant looks sick, it gets a dose of manure tea, and a new 
mulch of manure. | have found this a sure cure in our yard 
for rot in iris rhizomes, thrips in glads, and scale in lilacs. 
| haven't lost a birch tree since | started to mulch them 
with manure.
