it done when the “propér” equipment is not available. Most 
conceivable snafus are dealt with. Not just how to use a 
dozer so it doesn’t turn over, and rig a winch so it doesn’t 
snarl; but how to get your dozer back on its treads without 
calling the AAA, and how to unsnarl your winch. All is 
explained in text and again in eminently understandable 
drawings.
