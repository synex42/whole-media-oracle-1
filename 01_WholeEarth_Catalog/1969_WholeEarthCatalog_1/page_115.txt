number rating according to (a) its frequency of inclusion in standard reference book selection guides and bibliographies, (b) where too new 
to be included in such guides, its compliance with accepted evaluative criteria, and (c) its cost in comparison with other similar and equally 
rated works. Where two or more sets have been equally rated, they are listed by order of cost within their ratings.
