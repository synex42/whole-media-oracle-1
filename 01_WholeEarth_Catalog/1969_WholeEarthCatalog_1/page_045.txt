textile industry. BASF of Germany is said by the 
American Chemical Society to be well along in de- 
velopment of a process that will “cast” fabrics directly

is correct, much of today’s $15-billion worth of fiber- 
production and textile-mill equipment could become 
obsolete almost overnight, and an entirely new type 
of plant, with entirely new equipment, will be needed.
