For general purpose high-quality mobile recording, the Uher 
4000 L is still is front of everyone else for reliability and 
economy (approx $440). 
The new Uher 1000 Report Pilot, 
designed for sound/film synchronization, costs about $695. 
Portable listening is another matter entirely than recording. 
Here I’m inclined to agree with Bob Fass that cassette is the 
medium and Sony the manufacturer. Specifically, the TC 124 
(stereo, w/o extra speakers): $149. 
The top of the heap in sound recording is Nagra. 
Their prices start at $1123.
