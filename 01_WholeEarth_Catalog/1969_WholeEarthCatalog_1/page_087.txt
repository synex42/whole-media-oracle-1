OBSTETRICAL TABLE TO ESTIMATE WHEN BABY WILL BE BORN 
The date on the top line is the first day of the LAST menstruation before pregnancy. The date 
immediately underneath it is the theoretical date when the baby should be born. Practically, 
however, the theoretical date will rarely be hit. The baby will usually arrive within two weeks 
before or two weeks after this date. Thus, if the date of the last menstruation was Jan. 1, the 
baby will be born somewhere between September 24 and October 23.
