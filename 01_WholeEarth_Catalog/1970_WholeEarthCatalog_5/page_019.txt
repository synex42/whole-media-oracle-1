Our final culture probably will have to contain (as certain 
Eskimo and Indian cultures have) ceremonies of death to 
be entered into voluntarily by the aged. On a planet so 
crowded we probably cannot live out the three-score-ten 
we've been conditioned to expect; we shall probably have 
to remove ourselves at two-score-ten or even earlier. The 
culture will have to evolve an agreement that living more 
than a certain number of years is thoughtless.
