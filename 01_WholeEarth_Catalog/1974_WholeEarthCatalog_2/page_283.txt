Here are 48 experiments for either kitchen or classroom that 
will help kids learn to generalize from cause and effect. 
Experiments are grouped in seven sections: air, carbon 
dioxide, heat, crystals, magnetism, electricity, and sound. 
Photos of multi-ethnic groups add to the book’s appeal. 
Help for the non-expert teacher/parent: An appendix of 
brief paragraphs explaining scientific principles behind 
each experiment.
