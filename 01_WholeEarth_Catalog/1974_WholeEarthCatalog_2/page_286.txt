~The Ascent of Man 
What were the pivotal events in the “ascent of man?” In 
_ his newest book, Jacob Bronowski—— a most unusual 
scientist—— traces the discoveries that have shaped man’s 
cultural, intellectual and technological development. In 
_ brilliant style he dissects the entire developmental process 
and assembles it into 
anew whole. One reason the book 
Succeeds so well in tracing these intricate patterns, is the 
clarity with which Bronowski speaks—— anyone can follow 
him through the most difficult discussions. Another plus 
is the lavish illustrations (scenes from the film series upon 
which the book is based). 
If ever there were a gap between the two cultures, the 
sciences and the humanities, Bronowski bridges it by 
synthesizing both into one intellectual system. His attitude 
- toward science is crucial: ““You must see that in a sense all 
science, all human thought, is a form of play;” “there 
cannot be a philosophy, there cannot even be a decent 
science, without humanity.’’ With authority Bronowski 
describes a science based on humanism, and his positive 
outlook is contagious. Read this book: it will give youa 
new vision, perhaps an encouraging one. 
—John Vollmer
