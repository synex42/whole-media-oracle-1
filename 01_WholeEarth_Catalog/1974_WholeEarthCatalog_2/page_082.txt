trying to raise less than a hundred gallons to 140° F. By 
circulating a large volume of water rapidly through the 
solar collector, thereby keeping the collector cool, the Pool 
Heater can take full advantage of the fact that solar

Almost universally, high-temp solar collectors are made 
of metal to withstand the temperatures generated by the 
greenhouse effect. High-performance solar collectors 
with black, selective surface coatings and multi-level 
glazing experience temperatures well above 300° F. 
However, because collectors for Solar Pool Heaters do 
not have to withstand such high temperatures, they can 
be made out of plastic.
