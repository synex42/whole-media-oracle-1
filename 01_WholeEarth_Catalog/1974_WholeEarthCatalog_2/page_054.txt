How to Figure Actual Nitrogen 
Fertilizing recommendations often call for a certain amount 
of actual nitrogen (as in, “apply 1 pound actual nitrogen 
per 1,000 square feet’’). Here is how to calculate actual 
nitrogen or actual anything-else: 
e 
Look for the percentage of nitrogen (N) on the 
fertilizer label. 
e 
Multiply that percentage times the weight of the 
fertilizer. 
For example, 10 pounds of blood meal 
(13 percent nitrogen) contains 1.3 pounds actual 
nitrogen.
