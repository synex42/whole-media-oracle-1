Heat Pipes 
Heat pipes are one of the more fascinating and promising technological 
wonders. 
They look like a simple pipe, have no moving parts, and need 
not be made from exotic materials. 
They take heat from one end to 
the other with hardly any losses, and thus have a great potential for 
raising the efficiency of many thermal devices. For example, an array of 
heat pipes in a chimney could transfer that wasted heat to the room.
