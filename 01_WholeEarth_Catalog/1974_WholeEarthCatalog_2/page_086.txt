RESEARCH COMMUNITIES 
We've been led to believe that invention comes eithe 
from lone men in their basements or gaudy 
corporation and university laboratories, and nowher 
between. Crash Government funding for energy- 
relief is being dispensed accordingly. What a waste. 
In OUR experience, commune-suspicious as we are, 
nearly all the best work in alternative energy tech- 
nology has come from small groups. They generally 
consist of a few families, a focussing spokesman, a 
rich assortment of talented multi-specialists (some 
of whom migrate from group to group), a central 
office and numerous field projects, and a nourishing 
network of collaborators, funders, customers, and 
audience. By some primary commitment to 
excellence these groups stabilize in a rapid learning 
mode. 
Their products are hungry-ingenious and 
honed fine. 
}
Our favorites are (in order of origin) Intermediate 
Technology Development Group, Zomeworks, The 
New Alchemy Institute, and Integrated Living 
Systems. Excellent material has come from

we’re unfamiliar with how they work. Project 
Ouroboros /ooks promising. Community Technology
