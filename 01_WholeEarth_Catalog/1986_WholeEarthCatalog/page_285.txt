Rowing Boats 
These needlecraft are a far cry from the traditional sea- 
worthy workboats that have graced our shores for centuries. 
You can still get the older styles, some in modern materials. 
But lately there has been a great surge of creativity in 
long, thin shells that practically fly over the water. Rowing 
is great aerobic training too, incomparably more aesthetic 
than (ugh) rowing machines. These two dealers carry a 
variety of equipment. Their expertise is boundless.
