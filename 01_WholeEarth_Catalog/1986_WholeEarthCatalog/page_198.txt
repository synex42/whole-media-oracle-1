Profits from pushcart vending largely depend on the nature 
of your product and the location of your cart, as well as 
the season of the year. An average day’s sales in Boulder, 
Colorado, in 1982 ran about $150, with a good day 
bringing in over $300 for some. With low overhead and 
minimal supply costs, most of that is profit, but pushcart 
vending will never make you rich. If you’re planning on 
making your primary income from pushcart vending, 
you'll probably need to place several carts around town.
