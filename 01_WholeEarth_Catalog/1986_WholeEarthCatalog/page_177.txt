What is Skiving? 
: 
Skiving is the process of reducing the thickness of leather, 
of actually slicing away negative leather from the 
original thickness. Skiving is a functional procedure 
rather than a decorative embellishment. 
. 
. 
. 
While reducing thickness, the process of skiving 
also makes leather more flexible. Skiving is therefore used 
whenever you run up against any thickness problem in 
folding, creasing, flexing, or edge turning. 
—Modern Leather Design
