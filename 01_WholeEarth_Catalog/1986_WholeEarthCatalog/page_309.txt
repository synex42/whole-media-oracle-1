Catalog free from 2 Swamp Road, Princeton, WI 54968. 
Kitchen Sink has one foot in the underground camp and

Eclipse Comics 
Catalog free with SASE from P. O. Box 199, 
Guerneville, CA 95446. 
These folks straddle a lot of territory with their 
comics. Their superheroes (such as DNA Agents) 
are pretty standard fare, but they’ve got other 
items such as a mini-series from P. Craig Russell 
that stands above the herd. Recently, Eclipse has 
been in the forefront of the 3-D comic revival, 
with nicely produced 3-D editions of several 
of their titles.
