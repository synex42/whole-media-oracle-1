To those working to solve city problems, he demonstrates the 
application of the ecological view to the problem of distributing 
open space in a metropolitan region. He recommends the 
creation of urban airsheds as responses to atmospheric pollution

of open space penetrating from the rural hinterlands, radially 
into the city.
