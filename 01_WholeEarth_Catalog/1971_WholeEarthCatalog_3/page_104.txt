Kilns: 
If you're into gas kilns, build your own. Don't buy a 
gas kiln, cause you'll be ripped off by its overwhelming 
price. 
You can get 1000 K-23 insulating fire brick for 
about $300, or less if you shop around for secands. | got 
1000 of these for half price.

out of standard pipe fittings and use a vacuum cleaner for 
forced air.
