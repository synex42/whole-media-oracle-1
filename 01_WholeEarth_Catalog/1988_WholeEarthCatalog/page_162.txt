Clark Color Labs 
Information free from: P.O. Box 96300, 
Washington, DC 20090. 
For ten years I’ve been sending my 
film by mail to Clark Color Labs to 
get it developed. They're fast, cheap 
and good. They give you credit for 
unprintable negatives, offer you two 
prints for just slightly more than one, 
have labs all over the country, and 
print on Kodak paper. Their prices 
are the cheapest I've ever found. if 
you like to see your pictures the 
same day you finish a roll, this isn’t 
for you. But if, like mine, your roll of 
film has been in your camera a few 
months anyway, what’s one more 
week? 
— Cindy Fugett
