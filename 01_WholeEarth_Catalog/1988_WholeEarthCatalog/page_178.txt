No matter how small your living space, set boundaries 
between home and work, and keep them. Whether you 
have a room, closet, or a drawer, you need to have a 
place where your business things can remain 
undisturbed. |n addition, you'll escape the stresses of the 
business more easily if you can put the traces away and 
turn your attention fully to home life. Your family also 
needs room to maintain a normal family life. Your kids 
will be more supportive of your work if they are not 
constantly confronting and adapting to it. 
The bottom line to a home business isn't just financial. 
Pursuing a home business allows you to do what's really 
important to you as both a parent and an individual. A 
home business can help you live the way you think you 
should, while letting you follow your dreams... and isn’t 
that what success is all about?
