Love and Rockets 
From Fantagraphics 
Arguably the finest regular comic now 
being published. Written and drawn by 
the Hernandez brothers, Love and 
Rockets combines classic comic art with 
scripts worthy of Gabriel Garcia 
Marquez. Plus it has the best female 
characters in comics today. Seek this 
one out!
