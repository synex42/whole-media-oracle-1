Indian Uses of Native Plants 
This book is more than a guide to medicinal plants used by 
the California Indians. 
It is a log of ceremonies, customs, 
feasts, and the Indian culture as it existed at that time. 
Topics include: Hair; Tepees; Ceremonies & Magic; 
Tanning Hides; and so on. 
The book offers an understanding of how the Indian here 
dealt with his environment and made the very best of it.
