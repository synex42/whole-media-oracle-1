TWO LENSES IN ONE 
Light and Vision 
Bifocals—introduced to Americain the 18th Century 
by Kenjamin Franklin—help older people with rigid 
lens structure to focus at both near and far distances. 
The upper half of the spectacle lens gives slight cor- 
rection for distant viewing. The lower half is for 
close-up work; it provides the increased refraction 
needed to compensate for the increasing rigidity— 
and the inability to focus—of the aging lens.
