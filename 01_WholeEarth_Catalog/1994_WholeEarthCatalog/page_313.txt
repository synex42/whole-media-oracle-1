As someone who used to hate to bal- 
ance my checkbook, the thought of us- 
ing a program to keep my finances or- 
ganized took some getting used to. 
Then | discovered Quicken. It is hard to 
find a more popular program; almost 
everyone loves it. 
Quicken does much more than just 
keep track of your personal income 
expenses and net worth. It can write 
checks as you enter them in your 
records, pay bills electronically, and 
help you transfer money between your 
different accounts. Quicken lets you be 
compulsive or loose about how you 
enter your checking and credit card in- 
formation. You can specify tax catego- 
ries for all checks and payments when 
you enter them, or do it at the end of 
the year, or skip it altogether — then 
go back later, be more specific, and 
generate new reports. 
— Paul Hoffman
