that “systems tend to oppose their proper 
functions.” The ideal proper function of the 
American system is life, liberty, and the 
pursuit of happiness. Yet as it gropes its 
way into the 2Ist century, the system in 
reality increasingly endangers human life, 
denies personal liberty, and represses 
individual happiness.
