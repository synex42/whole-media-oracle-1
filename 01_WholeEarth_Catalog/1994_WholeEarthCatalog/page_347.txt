The Nature Conservancy

mental work, The Nature Conservancy offers 
trips for folks who wish to see what needs to 
be, or is being, conserved. They offer inspiring 
field trips and tours to a variety of locations in 
the States and Latin America. Conservancy 
members and local guides lead the trips, mak- 
ing sure that the proper sensitivities and atti- 
tudes are celebrated. Many find that it is a 
special pleasure to travel and leam in a group 
with similar concerns. You'll be helping out a 
bit, too; part of your fee contributes to Nature 
Conservancy's efforts. —J. Baldwin
