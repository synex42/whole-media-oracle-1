Study others to become skillful. 
Study yourself to become wise. 
What else is there to study? 
Act on others to have influence. 
Act on yourself to have strength. 
Can you focus totally on the task at hand? 
Expressing your needs is one thing. 
Can you see and hear and touch your surroundings with an open mind? 
Working to achieve them is quite another. 
Can you disregard your own convenience? 
Don't be distracted from your purpose. 
Can you feel and act naturally without obsessing with oughts and shoulds? 
Don't lose yourself in rumination. 
Whatever change occurs without flying away can you sit on the eggs of Reality? 
Can you see clearly enough to know when nonaction is action? 
Just offering, however accepted. 
Just growing vast while growing small. 
Just doing Reality’s work.
