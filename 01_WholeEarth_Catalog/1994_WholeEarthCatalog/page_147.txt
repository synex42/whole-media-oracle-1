the adventures: enthusiastic, front-line

We have no word in English to express Hei- 
mat in German, or bro or cynefin in Welsh, 
meaning familiar territory. But Common 
Ground is offering the word “parish” as a 
substitute. A parish, often based on ancient 
ecclesiastical delineations, is the smallest 
political and administrative unit recognized 
by central government in the United King- 
dom. But Common Ground is using the 
word in a looser sense to mean the locality 
to which people feel a sense of belonging 
and which belongs to them. People can draw 
their own lines, or use old ecclesiastical or 
newer civil parish or community council 
boundaries.
