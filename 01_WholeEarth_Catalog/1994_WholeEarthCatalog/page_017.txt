Whatever Happened to Natural History? 
“The secondhandedness of the learned world is the secret of its mediocrity.” 
—Alfred North Whitehead 
Molecular biology, experimental biology, and simulation modeling sucked science 
indoors, stashing natural history in a dusty glass display case. “Real” scientists pooh- 
poohed outdoor observational biology as “without controls.” Physics envy battered 
naturalists and ecologists who just wanted to know the style and scatter of bluebird 
nesting sites. Darwin was “real” only in the form of ahistorical mathematical models.
