In our everyday lives we take the role of 
plants almost entirely for granted. If we are 
fortunate enough to live in the country, the 
changing seasons may attract our attention; 
in towns, we may wander to the park. But 
the cornflakes on our breakfast table are 
unlikely to remind us of the maize plant, 
the toast hardly suggests the blowing wheat 
fields... . In truth, we seldom have direct 
contact with the plants on which our com- 
fort depends, or any experience of the la- 
bor and skill needed to make use of them.
