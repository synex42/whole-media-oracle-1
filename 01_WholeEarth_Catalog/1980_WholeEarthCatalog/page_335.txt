Using the Creeper 
Place child on back board. 
Fasten the harness — sides, 
tail, and top. Tighten the straps. Turn the frame over so 
that the child is suspended from the frame. 
If the child’s 
knees do not touch the floor, loosen the straps. 
If the 
child is too heavy to turn over while strapped in the frame 
have the child kneel and position the frame over his body. 
Buckle the harness in place. 
As the child grows, the creeper can be made higher by 
bolting on longer legs.
