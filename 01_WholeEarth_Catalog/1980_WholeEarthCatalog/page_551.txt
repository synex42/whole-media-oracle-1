Cardboard Racing Turtles 
My mother introduced this game to us as children and I’ve 
never seen it played anywhere. Yet it is so simple and 
such fun especially when grown-ups join in. 
Use: A shirt cardboard. Trace a dish circle, draw head, 
tail, arms and legs. 
Decorate with crayons or paint — make both sides like 
the front. 
Cut out the turtle. Make string hole. Measure 10’ of 
string. Tie one end of string to table leg. Thread string 
through turtle. Pull string taut, and turtle will stand. 
; 
Release and he'll flop forward. Pull-flop to the top of 
the string, flip him over and pull-flop home. Make 3. 
Find 2 friends and have a race.
