The way to prevent the other riders from knowing when 
you are suffering is to keep your mouth closed and look 
alert. 
Inside, your guts may be falling out, but you look 
fresh as a daisy. 
Sometimes, on the other hand, it’s beneficial to appear 
tired. You can pull a lot on your bars and wrestle with 
your bike. Breathe hard and try to look red in the face. 
People will notice this. They are watching and their eyes 
are flicking around all the time.
