Improving Energy Efficiency In Buildings 
Proper management of a building can greatly reduce its 
energy needs. 
This book is a manager's guide to auditing 
the building’s energy use, its leaks, and its energy-eating 
flaws. A good bit of the book is devoted to what to do 
about what the audit finds. Though intended more for 
factories and large structures than houses, the same prin- 
ciples apply. If you have a big building in your life, a 
Jook at this professional text will probably save you lots 
of money. 
—J. Baldwin
