Throughout 228 carefully laid out pages, Joe Up- 
ton’s journal is beautifully written, an extraordinary work 
of love; a marvelous mating of time, place, mood and lore. 
Specifically his subject is salmon trolling, with lesser 
attention paid to gill netting, long lining and market 
doings. As the central issue is conservation, his conclu-

along a thousand miles of our last wilderness.
