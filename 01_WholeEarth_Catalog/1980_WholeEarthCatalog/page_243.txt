While wood is the basic component of the PSP system, 
polyethylene is the secret of its success. Polyethylene is 
inexpensive, easy to work with, and readily available. 
It 
is an absolute moisture barrier and is what keeps the 
wooden walls from rotting. While it is true that this 
plastic deteriorates quickly when exposed to the ultra- 
violet rays of sunlight, it lasts indefinitely underground.
