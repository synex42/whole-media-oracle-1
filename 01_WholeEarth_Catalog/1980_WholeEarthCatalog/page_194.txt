coating that is transparent to light and short wave infra-

It 
is, in effect, transparent insulation. Because heat mirror 
is not particularly durable when exposed to weather, it 
is available only as an inner layer in commercially avail- 
able multipane windows. 
It works well, as | can attest 
from personal experience.
