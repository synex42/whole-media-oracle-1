Adhesive Engineering Company 
These guys are doing what construction people never 
figured could be done. They take structures like collapsed 
concrete bridges, jack them back into place, inject some 
adhesive, and the bridge is stronger than before it collapsed. 
They print a pamphlet called Construction Adhesives 
Coatings and Sealants, and they provide information and 
survey to about every possible application of industrial 
gluing. Alan Kalker suggested it, through Herb Grubb. 
Alan’s idea was that there might be an application here 
for prepoured concrete dome sections. Just pour the 
sections, hoist them in place, and stick them together.
