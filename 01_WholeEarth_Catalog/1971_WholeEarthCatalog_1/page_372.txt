Ladybird Books 
I come from a family that reads a lot. I had two children, aged 5 
and 6% who could not read at all. I had pointed to their TOES 
and shown them all the Dolman words and read Beginner books 
and Dr. Seuss at them. One day an acquaintance of ours came for 
lunch and brought his 5 year old daughter. She found the Beginner 
books and the Dr. Seuss and read them to herself. We found out that 
she learned to read on Ladybird books. 
We started our kids the 
next day, September 17. By January 4 of the next year the 5 year 
old was on book 5b, and the 6% year old on Book 11a, that is, the 
younger child could read books of the Beginner book variety, and 
the older one could cope with just about any children’s book. 
We 
read to them from the books for %2 hour each night, and answered 
the questions. 
We saw to it that they got books as they were 
ready for them. That’s all we did. So in 3% months they learned 
to read, substantially by themselves.
