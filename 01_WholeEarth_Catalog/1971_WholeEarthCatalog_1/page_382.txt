noises, horrible faces and kick and wave your arms frantically. 
None of these moves will do you the slightest good. Now is the

drownproofing work: 
Whether your nose and throat are full of water from lips to 
stomach, the stroke or the kick will always get you to the 
surface... 
If you do it in the same manner as you had been doing it, :f 
you blow out through your nose, with your lips clamped 
tightly together as you break the surface with another kick

If your eyes are open and if you inhale through your mouth, 
whether you end up with all air, half air and half foam, you 
will be better off, and if you continue the cycle,no matier 
how much water you shipped, things will get better much soon:

If this procedure is stuck to and mastered, 90 percent of those

cycle properly, you are-sure to be all right. What counts is that 
you get yourself out of trouble.
