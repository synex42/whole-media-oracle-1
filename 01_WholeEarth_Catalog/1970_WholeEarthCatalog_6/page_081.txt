embellishment that naturally appeals to me as a sculptor ...1! can’t 
really say what first attracted me to dulcimers, the shape or the

different art forms, and the same individuals will change with time 
and experience, | have found that a close experience with function 
has enhanced my initial appreciation of form, until now | enjoy a 
well-designed musical instrument as much as | do any other art 
object. To say this in more concrete terms, | have found that 
| like building instruments because it involves the visual, tactile,
