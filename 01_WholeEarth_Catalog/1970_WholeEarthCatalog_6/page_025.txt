that was discussing how to bury various 
items in a recent issue, that you need not 
put oil in the container around the tools, 
all that is necessary-is to seal them until 
the air can’t get to them, it is the moisture 
in the air that rusts anything.

nple take a new outboard motor, drop it 
a lake in five years go back, get it out, 
will be like it was brand new, but it 
will rust in minutes if not bathed in oil. 
I would say the best way to bury small 
items like knives would be to put them in 
mason jars, seal them and put a small
