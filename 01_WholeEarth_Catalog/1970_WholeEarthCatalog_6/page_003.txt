THE COVER 
The snapshot of the universe on the front cover was 
made one August afternoon this summer a few miles 
east of Albuquerque with a 180° fisheye lens. The 
composite photo on the back cover, done by Prof. 
Bart Bok of Steward Observatory at Tuscon, was 
sent to us by Carl Sagan in response to our plaint 
for a photograph of the Whole galaxy last spring. 
The quote is from Sagan’s introduction to his 
Planetary Exploration, 7970, 76pp. $2.00 from 
University of Oregon Books, 107 Friendly Hall, 
University of Oregon, Eugene, Oregon 97403. 
Cover designed by Stewart Brand.
